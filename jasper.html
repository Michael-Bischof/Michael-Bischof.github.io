Jasper

The Coin Hunter

Welcome, this is the website for the hyper casual game "Jasper - The Coin Hunter"!

It almost feels like the App Store does not have a single decent game anymore that is free and not filled with crazy amounts of ads. Since I could not find any good ones anymore, I decided to create a hyper casual game like the ones I loved back in the day.

Jasper is inspired by Snake, Doodle Jump, Tiny Wings, Flappy Bird, Crossy Roads and probably quite a few more games that where just... fun. No forced 30s fullscreen ads. No payed subscription to get more coins. No skins for ridiculous amounts of real money.

Maybe some other people will also appreciate a game like they used to be ❤️

Please contact me via email if there is anything I can help you with.
